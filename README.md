## Date Wheel: Days Calculator

**[Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6774676322)**

A fast, elegant workdays calculator. Spin the wheel to pick any two dates and instantly see the number of effective working days between them — weekends excluded, public holidays respected, and your own custom off-days supported.

### Features
- Working-days count between any two dates
- Public holiday calendar for 90+ countries and regions
- State/province-level holidays (US, Canada, Australia, Germany, and more)
- Add your own recurring days off (e.g. every other Friday)
- Add one-off custom off dates

## Support

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: My country's holidays aren't showing.**
A: Tap the flag chip in the top-left → select your country. Holidays load from the internet; make sure you have a connection on first use.

**Q: How do I add a day off that repeats every other week?**
A: Tap the calendar icon → Custom Off Days → + → enable "Every other week" and pick the weekday and date range.

**Q: My custom off-days disappeared after reinstalling.**
A: Custom data is stored locally. Reinstalling the app clears local storage — this is an iOS limitation.

**Q: The working-day count seems wrong.**
A: Check that you've selected the correct country and state/province. Some regions have unique public holidays that differ from the national defaults.

**Q: Can I use the app without an internet connection?**
A: Yes — once holiday data has been fetched for a country/year it is cached on-device and works offline.
