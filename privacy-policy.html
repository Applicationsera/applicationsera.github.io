Privacy Policy

App: Photo Booth - Photo Editor
Last updated: June 16, 2025

1. No Data Collection

Photo Booth does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All photo editing happens entirely on your device.

2. No Internet Required

The app works fully offline. Your photos never leave your iPhone or iPad.

3. Photos & Camera

We request access to your photo library and camera solely to let you select and edit photos within the app. We do not access, copy, or upload your photos.

4. No Third-Party SDKs

Photo Booth does not include any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

5. Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

6. Contact

Questions? Reach us via the in-app feedback button or at applicationsera.github.io.
